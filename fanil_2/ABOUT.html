About TERRA

Can your old clothing improve the future? Yes!
At TERRA, we recycle your used garments through our circular sustainability programs.
Take our recycled down initiative. We collect TERRA down garments from all over the
world and transform them into new products. We also work with NGOs and partners to
distribute wearable used clothing to refugees, disaster victims, and others in need
worldwide. At TERRA, we believe in the power of clothing to create a better tomorrow.
Our clothes recycling is just one part of our sustainability efforts to RECYCLE, REUSE,
